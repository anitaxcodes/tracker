Protein Shake Replacements 🍃
The original plan uses protein shakes 4 times a day. Here is what to eat instead with Nepali household food — same protein, zero cost.
Shake Replacement Option 1
1 glass warm milk + 2 tbsp sattu stirred in + pinch black salt
→ approx 18-20g protein. Use this in the morning and mid-morning slot.
Shake Replacement Option 2
1 boiled egg + small bowl plain curd (dahi) + 1 tbsp sattu
→ approx 18g protein. Use this for lunch replacement on no-carb days.
Shake Replacement Option 3
100g firm tofu pan-fried with salt and turmeric + 1 glass milk
→ approx 17g protein. Use this for evening snack slot.
Shake Replacement Option 4
1 cup soya milk (homemade or packed) + 1 boiled egg
→ approx 15g protein. Use this at night before the kitchen closes.
Foods Allowed on No-Carb Days
✓ Eggs (boiled, scrambled, bhurji) — 1 egg per day only
✓ Tofu (pan fried, no sauce)
✓ Plain curd / dahi
✓ Sattu in water or milk
✓ Milk (warm, no sugar)
✓ Chicken (boiled or grilled, no marinade with sugar)
✓ Spinach, cabbage, cucumber, tomato, beans
✓ Dal (only on day 4 onward, small portion)
✓ Mustard oil (tiny amounts for cooking only)
✓ Salt, turmeric, jeera, green chilli, garlic, ginger
Strictly Avoid All 28 Days
✗ Rice, roti, bread, noodles, pasta
✗ Sugar in any form including in tea
✗ Biscuits, chips, namkeen, processed snacks
✗ Alcohol
✗ Fruit juice (eat whole fruit only)
✗ Fried anything (tarkari must be dry or steamed)
✗ Milk tea with sugar (lemon tea or black tea only)
Daily Rules Every Day
💧 Drink 2.5 to 3 litres of water daily
⏰️ Finish your last meal 4 hours before sleeping
🌿 Eat every 3 to 4 hours — do not starve
⚠️ Low BP rule: always carry 4 almonds when going out
🌿 Lemon water first thing every morning
😋 Your one black tea per day is allowed, no sugar
Expected result: 3 to 5 kg in 28 days if followed consistently. The first 3 days are hardest. After day 4 it becomes manageable. After week 2 you will notice clothes fitting differently even before the scale moves much.
